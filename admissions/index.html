Eligibility Criteria

Pre-Primary (Nursery to KG)
Age requirements for pre-primary classes
  • Nursery: 3+ years as of 31st March
  • LKG: 4+ years as of 31st March
  • UKG: 5+ years as of 31st March
Primary (Classes I-V)
Requirements for primary classes
  • Age appropriate for the class applied
  • Successful completion of previous class
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
Middle & Secondary (VI-XII)
Requirements for higher classes
  • Minimum 60% marks in previous class
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Character Certificate
  • Report Card of previous academic year

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Birth Certificate (Original + Copy)
  • Transfer Certificate (Original)
  • Report Card of Previous Class
  • Passport Size Photographs (4)
  • Aadhar Card (Copy)
  • Residential Proof (Copy)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process for new students?

The admission process involves five simple steps:
  • Submit the application form with required documents
  • Complete the entrance assessment
  • Attend parent-student interview
  • Receive admission confirmation
  • Complete fee payment

What is the age requirement for Nursery admission?

For Nursery admission, the child should be at least 3 years old as of 31st March of the academic year.

Is transportation facility available?

Yes, we provide transportation facilities for students from various locations in and around Dhuri. The transportation fee varies based on the distance from the school.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a student-teacher ratio of 20:1 to ensure personalized attention and effective classroom management.

Can I apply for mid-session admission?

Yes, mid-session admissions are considered subject to seat availability and meeting the eligibility criteria. Please contact the admission office for more information.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer a wide range of activities including:
  • Sports and athletics
  • Music and dance
  • Art and craft
  • Debate and public speaking
